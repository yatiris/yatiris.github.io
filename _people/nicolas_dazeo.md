### Bio

Nicol치s Dazeo (Nico) is an Assistant Researcher at CNEA. He holds a PhD in Computational and Industrial Mathematics from UNICEN (2022), which was funded by a scholarship granted by CONICET. He also served as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the same institution. His research focuses on the computational modeling of human hemodynamics, computer-assisted vascular interventions, and web technologies. He is also a Teaching Assistant at UNICEN.
